# klub

Emerald-led brand with cyan secondary. Bright, inviting, classical typography.

## Identity

- **Primary font:** Inter (fallback: Test Calibre, system-ui)
- **Primary color:** `emerald-500`
- **Secondary color:** `cyan-500`
- **Type scale:** standard (16/14/12)
- **Label weight:** 600

## When to use

The Klub! product surface — community / club-management features.
